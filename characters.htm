Characters

In brawl stars, there is a total of 27 characters, and there are five rarities in which they come from. Common, Rare, Super Rare, Mythic, and Legendary. There are also characters that are unlocked on Trophy Road, which is a trophy reward system where when you get enough trophies, you unlock achievements. However, Trophy Road characters fall into the Common rarity, because they can not be unlocked any other way. 9 of the 27 characters are Common, which include: Shelly, Nita. Colt, Bull, Jessie, Brock, Dynamike, Bo, and Tick. The next 4 characters are Rare. They include: El Primo, Barley, Poco, and Rosa. Next on the list come the Super Rare brawlers, which are: Rico, Darryl, Penny, and Carl. Furthermore, the Mythic brawlers include: Mortis, Tara, and Gene. Last come the Legendary characters, which include: Spike, Crow, and Leon. Common brawlers are the easiest to obtain, and Legendary brawlers arfe the toughest to obtain.

Shelly

Rarity: Common

Shelly is a Common Bralwer that is unlocked when beginning Brawl Stars. She has average health and damage. She does more damage if more bullets hit, so she is stronger at closer range. However, Shelly is a mid-range brawler, as her range is average. Hitting enemies charges her super, and her super is a giant shot that can clear obstacles as well as stun and knock back enemies. Shelly has two star powers. Her first one, "Shell Shock", slows down enemies for 3.0 seconds when they are hit by her super. Her second star power, "Band Aid", instantly returns Shelly to full health when she falls below 40%. It charges over the course of 20.0 seconds. At power level 1, Shelly has 3600 health, and does 300 damage per shell. At power level 10, or max level, Shelly has 5040 health, and does 420 damage per shell.

Nita

Rarity: Common

Nita is a Common Trophy Road Brawler that is unlocked upon reaching 10 trophies. Her attack is a shockwave, damaging multiple enemies that are caught in the tremor. Nita is a mid-range brawler as her range is average. Her super summons a bear that has high health and attacks using quick, close range swipes. Nita has two star powers. Her first one, "Bear With Me", heals 500 health on her bear when she lands a shot on an enemy, and the bear heals 500 health for her when it lands attacks on an enemy. Her second star power, "Hyper Bear", makes the attack speed of her bear twice as fast. At power level 1, Nita has 3800 health and does 740 damage per shot. Her bear has 4000 health, and deals 400 damage per slash.At power level 10, or max level, Nita has 5320 health, and does 1036 damage per shot. Her bear has 5600 health, and deals 560 damage per slash.

Colt

Rarity: Common

Colt is a Common Trophy Road Brawler that is unlocked upon reaching 60 trophies. He has lower than average health, but has rather high damage. He is along range brawler, which means at further distance he has an advantage over close range brawlers. His super is a extra long volley of shots that break obstacles. Colt has two star powers. His first one, "Slick Boots", increases his movement speed by 8%, allowing to move faster than most other brawlers. His second star power, "Magnum Special", increases his attack range and bullet speed by 11%, allowing his shots to become faster and more rangy. At power level 1, Colt has 2800 health and does 300 damage per bullet. At power level 10, or max level, Colt has 3920 health, and does 420 damage per bullet.

Bull

Rarity: Common

Bull is a Common Trophy Road Brawler that is unlocked upon reaching 250 trophies. He has high health and high damage, but he is an extremely close range brawler, so he will not be able to damage long range or mid range brawlers unless he is right next to them. Bull's super causes him to charge forward for quite a big distance, destroying obstacles and damaging enemies if they are hit by his charge. Bull has two star powers. His first one, "Berserker", doubles his attack speed when he falls below 40% health. His second star power, "Tough Guy", gives him a shield that reduces all damage by 30% when he falls below 40% health. At power level 1, Bull has 5200 health, and does 400 damage per shell. At power level 10, or max level, Bull has 7280 health, and does 560 damage per shell.

Jessie

Rarity: Common

Jessie is a Common Trophy Road Brawler that is unlocked upon reaching 500 trophies. She has average health and damage, and she is a pretty long range brawler. Her shots bounce off enemies for a total of 3 times, giving her shots potential to hit three enemies at once. Jessie's super places a turret with medium health and low damage. However, it fires with quick speed. She has once star power: "Energize", which allows her to repair her turret for 800 health as a time when she bounces her shot off it. At power level 1, Jessie has 3200 health and does 820 damage per shot. Her turret has 2800 health, and does 240 damage per shot. At power level 10, or max level, Jessie has 4480 health and does 1148 per shot. Her turret has 3920 health, and deals 336 damage per shot.

Brock

Rarity: Common

Brock is a Common Trophy Road Brawler that is unlocked upon reaching 1000 trophies. He has low health but semi-high damage, and he is an extremely long range brawler. His super is a brigade of rockets that rain from the sky, break obstacles, and damage targets in the range of his rocket air-strike. Brock has two star powers. His first one, "Incendiary", sets the ground on fire where his rocket lands, doing 300 damage per second. His second star power, "Rocket No. Four", allows him to have 4 ammo slots instead of the regular 3, allowing him to launch more rockets and deal more damage. At power level 1, Brock has 2800 health, and deals 1100 damage per rocket. At power level 10, or max level, Brock has 3920 health, and does 1540 damage per shot.

Dynamike

Rarity: Common

Dynamike is a Common Trophy Road Brawler thats is unlocked upon reaching 2000 trophies. He has low health but high damage. Dynamike is a thrower, which means unlike most other brawlers, his shots (dynamite sticks) can travel over walls. His super is a large bomb that deals damage in a large area and knocks back enemies that get hit. Dynamike has two star powers. His first one, "Dyna-Jump", allows Dynamike to jump when he is in range of his dynamite sticks. This allows him to jump over enemy attacks and obstacles. When Dyanmike is in the air, he is immune to all damage, except damage over time attacks, such as Crow poison. His second star power, "Demolition", adds 1000 damage to his super, allowing him to kill many brawlers that would not usually die to Dynamikes super. At power level 1, Dynamike has 2800 health, and does 760 damage per dynamite stick. At power level 10, or max level, Dynamike has 3920 health, and does 1064 damage per dynamite stick.

Bo

Rarity: Common

Bo is a Common Trophy Road Brawler that is unlocked upon reaching 3000 trophies. He is a mid-range brawler, and he has fairly high health and damage. His super places 3 mines that trigger when enemies step on them, and they momentarily stun them as well. Bo has one star power: "Circling Eagle", which allows him to see 4 tiles into bushes, instead of the regular 2 tiles that other brawlers usually can see in bushes. At power level 1, Bo has 3600 health, and deals 520 damage per arrow. At power level 10, or max level, Bo has 5040 health, and deals 728 damage per arrow.

Tick

Rarity: Common

Tick is a Common Trophy Road Brawler that is unlocked upon reaching 4000 trophies. He is a thrower, which means his attacks are able to travel over obstacles, unlike most other brawlers. He has low health and low damage, although his range is pretty far, allowing him to damage other brawlers at long range. Tick's super causes his head to fall off, and it runs to find an opponent, and when it dies or reaches one, explodes. Tick has one star power: "Well Oiled", which allows Tick to heal 2.0 seconds faster than other brawlers when he is not attacking or taking damage. At power level one, Tick has 2200 health, and deals 640 damage per mine. His super has 1600 health, and deals 2000 damage. At power level 10, or max level, Tick has 3080 health, and deals 896 damage per mine. His super has 2240 health and deals 2800 damage.

El Primo

Rarity: Rare

El Primo is a Rare Brawler that can be unlocked through boxes or purchase through the Brawl Stars Shop. He is a close-range brawler, dealing damage with his fists. He has high health and average damage. His super allows him to jump over brawlers and walls, and it deals damage if he lands on enemy brawlers. El Primo has one star power: "El Fuego", which causes fire over time damage that deals a total of 800 damage over the course of 4 seconds when enemies are caught in El Primo's jump. At power level 1, El Primo has 5800 health, and deals 360 damage per punch. At power level 10, or max level, El Primo has 8120 health and deals 504 damage per punch.

Barley

Rarity: Rare

Barley is a Rare Brawler that can be unlocked through boxes or purchase through the Brawl Stars Shop. He is a medium-range thrower, which means that his attacks travel over obstacles, unlike the attacks of most other brawlers. Barley's attacks also leave a mark on the ground, and they will deal damage over time if the opponent stays in the range of the marks of his attack. Barley's super is similar to his regular attack, except he throws 5 bottles, and they cover a very wide radius and amount of space. Barley has two star powers. His first one, "Medical Use", allows Barley to regain 300 health every time he attacks. His second star power, "Extra Noxious", adds 140 damage per second to Barley's attacks, allowing him to deal a higher amount of damage. At power level 1, Barley has 2400 health, and does 680 damage per second with his bottles. At power level 10, or max level, Barley has 3360 health, and deals 952 damage per second with his bottles.

Poco

Rarity: Rare

Poco is a Rare Brawler that can be unlocked through boxes or purchase through the Brawl Stars Shop. He is a medium-range brawler, who attacks by strumming his guitar, and his range is very wide. He has average health and damage, and his super heals himself and his teammates that are in his super's range. Poco has two star powers. His first one, "Da Capo!", allows him to heal his teammates for 500 health when he shoots them with his attack. His second star power, "Screeching Solo", allows his super to do a total of 1200 damage to enemies, when it originally doesn't damage enemies at all. At power level 1, Poco has 3800 health, and does 660 damage per shot. At power level 10, or max level, Poco has 5320 health, and deals 924 damage per shot.

Rosa

Rarity: Rare

Rosa is a Rare Brawler that can be unlocked through boxes or purchase through the Brawl Stars Shop. She is a close-range brawler who attacks using her planty boxing gloves. She has high health and medium damage. Her super gives her a shield that temporarily reduces all damage she takes by 70%. Rosa has two star powers. Her first one, "Plant Life", allows her to recover 200 health per second while she is sitting in a bush. Her second star power, "Thorny Gloves", allow her to gain 200 damage to her attacks while her super shield is activated. At power level 1, Rosa has 5000 health, and does 460 damage per punch. At power level 10, or max level, Rosa has 7000 health, and deals 644 damage per punch.

Rico

Rarity: Super Rare

Rico is a Super Rare Brawler that can be unlocked through boxes or purchase through the Brawl Stars Shop. He is a long-ranger brawler who's shots reflect off walls, allowing him to attack enemies that are hiding behind obstacles. His super is a giant release of bouncy balls that can penetrate through multiple enemies and damage many targets at once. Rico has two star powers. His first one, "Super Bouncy", causes Rico bullets that bouce off walls to deal 100 more damage than regular shots. His second star power, "Robo Retreat", allows Rico to run 34% faster when he falls below 40% health.

Darryl

Rarity: Super Rare

Darryl is a Super Rare Brawler that can be unlocked through boxes or purchase through the Brawl Stars Shop. He is a close-range brawler that fires two slower shots in one attack. Unlike other brawlers, Darryl's super slowly charges on it's own, without him having to actually attack other brawlers for it to charge. His super is a mid-ranged roll that allows him to charge on to enemies or roll away to escape. Darryl has one star power: "Steel Hoops". When Darryl uses his super, he gains a shield that reduces all damage he takes by 30% for 3.5 seconds. At power level 1, Darryl has 4600 health, and does 280 damage per shell. At power level 10, or max level, Darryl has 6440 health, and deals 392 damage per shell.

Penny

Rarity: Super Rare

Penny is a Super Rare Brawler that can be unlocked through boxes or purchase through the Brawl Stars Shop. She is a mid to long-range brawler, and her attack is a flying bag of money. However, unlike regular brawler attacks, Penny's money bag deals more damage to a target behind another target. So if an enemy brawler is standing behind another enemy brawler, and the enemy brawler in front gets hit, the enemy brawler behind will take much more damage. Penny's super is mortar, which launches bombs at the enemies. Penny has one star power: "Last Blast", which means that when Penny's mortar is destroyed or she places more than one at once, the dead mortar will launch a bunch of bombs and target at enemy brawlers. At power level 1, Penny has 3200 health, and deals 840 damage per shot. At power level 10, or max level, Penny has 4480 health, and deals 1176 damage per shot.

Carl

Rarity: Super Rare

Carl is a Super Rare Brawler that can be unlocked through boxes or purchase through the Brawl Stars Shop. He is a mid to long-range brawler, and his attack throws an axe that, if hits a wall, will come back to him faster than a full shot would. Unlike other brawlers, Carl only has 1 ammo slot, so he has to wait for his thrown axe to return before he can attack furthermore. Carl's super allows him to spin and move extremely quickly, and brawlers that are caught in his super take damage. Carl has two star powers. His first one, "Power Throw", allows Carl to throw his axe 13% faster, giving him the ability to attack quickly. His second star power, "Protective Pirouette", causes Carl to take 30% less damage while he is in his super. At power level 1, Carl has 4400 health, and does 580 damage per ax shot. At power level 10, or max level, Carl has 6160 health, and deals 812 damage per axe shot.
