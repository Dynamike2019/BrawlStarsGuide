A background on Brawl Stars


Brawl Stars is created by SuperCell, which is a Finnish mobile game development company based in Helsinki, Finland. It was founded in May 2010, and released its first game in 2011, Gunshine.net. However, Gunshine was shut down in 2012.SuperCell's current games consist of: Hay Day, released in 2012, Clash of Clans, released in 2012, Boom Beach, released in 2013, Clash Royale, released in 2016, and Brawl Stars, released in 2017, and globablly re-released on December 12, 2018. When Brawl Stars got globally released, it made more than $63 million in its first month.

Brawl Stars has a total of 27 characters, as well as a trophy reward system, and is still changing and being updated to this day.
