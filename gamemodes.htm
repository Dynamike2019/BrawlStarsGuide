Game modes

In Brawl Stars, there are 10 game modes: Gem Grab, Solo Showdown, Duo Showdown, Heist, Bounty, Brawl Ball, Seige, Robo Rumble, Big Game, and Boss Fight. When the player starts Brawl Stars, Gem Grab is already unlocked. Once the player reaches 30 trophies, Solo Showdown and Duo Showdown are unlocked. At 150 trophies, Daily Events are unlocked. Daily Events include: Heist, Bounty, and Brawl Ball. Later on at 350 tropphies, Ticket Events are unlocked, which are: Robo Rumble, Big Game, and Boss Fight. Finally, at 800 trophies, the one Special Event: Seige, is unlocked. Once 800 trophies are hit, all the game modes are unlocked.
